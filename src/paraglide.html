SAFE PARAGLIDE

Our suggestion about weather in Malaysia if you want to paraglide

  • Ideal wind between 2 and 15mph, wind speed above 18mph (29km/h) isn’t suitable for paragliding.
  • If wind speed changes more than 2 m/s in two minute period, it’s gusty so not suitable for paragliding.

  • Rain and Strom Clouds is extremely dangerous for paraglide.
  • Make sure the wind direction is from the suitable degree.
